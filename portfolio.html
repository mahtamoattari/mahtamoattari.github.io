Interad

At Interad, we work on different projects to develop online applications for users that will help them fulfill their financial needs with little or no help from financial institutions. Our apps address problems like saving up, getting mortgage, getting pre-qualification for mortgage, finding your ideal home and buying your home. Our main focus is user satisfaction, and we try to achieve that by including user experience concepts in every step of our design process.

Challenges

We usually start by determining our end-users and the problem that we are aiming to solve for them. Next, we use persona and storyboard to get a deeper understanding of their problem, and then come up with potential solution(s). Afterwards, we try to improve our solution from different perspective by having discussions and defining the user and system flows. In order to achieve this, we design a wireframe to be able to discuss the solution in details, as well as perform some user testing.

We usually go through any of the above stages as many times as needed, to get the desired result. Finally, we create mock-ups and then develop the idea. This process does not end at this point. We always request for feedback from our end-users to help us constantly improve our products, even after the development is done.

Ryerson (LS3)

Working on the Movision project at Ryerson was a lot of fun. The goal of the project was to offer a semantic based platform to search about movies, TV shows, actors, directors, etc., and also provide a semantic based recommendation system

Challenges

Our challenge was to inform the end-users about the new technology that was being used, whilst offering a familiar design to them, so that they are able to see what they expect.

Experience

I used Balsamiq to create the wireframe and shared my initial ideas for the interface with the rest of the team. After having an agreement and making the adjustment, I created a prototype to demonstrate the interface.

Research

We researched about new technologies like using semantic search to offer the best results for the users.

Tasks

Web design and development. Interaction design and testing, research about new technologies.
