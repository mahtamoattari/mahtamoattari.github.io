Don't you find this confusing? :S

One of the website that use a lot is Ticketmaster. If you've ever used this website you might know that you can add your favorite acts in your list of favorites so when what you like is happening near you, you'll be notified about it via email.

What is confusing for me is the button that they use for this matter. To be more specific it's the color of the button. So before you add your favorite let say artist, the button looks like what you see in the top picture. It's blue and it is explicitly telling you to add this artist to your list, but that is not the confusing part. After you do that the message changes to my favorite and the color turns to gray.

Now what is confusing to me is, let say one month later when I am checking some tickets and stuff and I see that gray button beside something that I like I click on it because the gray color is telling me that this function is not active (the artist is not in my list of favorites) and the moment I click on it I get the blue button and I realize, oh ya it was already in my list.
