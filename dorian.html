Short Bio

Mahta AKA Dorian, was born and raised in Iran. Later (in her 20s) she moved to Canada to follow her passion in music. She is a self-driven, artistic, fine, and independent musician/singer/songwriter, based in Toronto. She performs all around Toronto and takes every opportunity to collaborate with other local musicians. She believes in music as a very powerful form of art as well as a magical means to bring people together.

She constantly tries to stay true to her music and her audience, and almost all of the stories in her songs are true stories, based on her life. She says that music has changed her life for better from the very beginning, and her biggest dream is to have the same effect on people’s life through music. Dorian’s new single “Sing With Me”, reflects her feelings towards her new life that she is experiencing by moving to Canada (and after overcoming different obstacles in the new environment) and finally reaching the point of begin accepted as a musician and artist in the society that she has joined not so long ago.
